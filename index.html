10 Things I Hate About You

Synopsis

"10 Things I Hate About You" is a high school romantic comedy where a new student, Cameron, falls for Bianca, but can't date her until her rebellious sister, Kat, does. So, Cameron asks the help of Patrick to woo Kat, leading to unexpected twists and turns in their love lives.

Cast and Directors
  • Heath Ledger as Patrick Verona
  • Julia Stiles as Kat Stratford
  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Cameron James
  • Larisa Oleynik as Bianca Stratford
  • Director: Gil Junger

    • Movie Review

    "10 Things I Hate About You" is a charming and witty teen rom-com that captivates with its modern take on Shakespeare.

    Mr. & Mrs. Smith

    Synopsis

    A married couple are getting bored with their quiet domestic life. What they don't know, however, is that they're both assassins, secretly hopping the world and killing for hire. But their separate lives are about to collide when they find out their next target is each other.

    Cast and Directors
  • Angelina Jolie as Jane Smith
  • Brad Pitt as John Smith
  • Vince Vaughn as Eddie
  • Adam Brody as Benjamin Danz
  • Director: Doug Liman

    • Movie Review

    Honestly one of my favourite movies of all time and definitely a must watch. It's extremely entertaining with action, romance and a good storyline. There are many scenes that were exceptionally shot and I think its abundantly obvious that the acting was amazing. I highly recommend.

    - Maya Traven

    4.2 Stars



    Confessions of a Shopaholic

    Synopsis

    A college grad lands a job as a financial journalist in New York City to support where she nurtures her shopping addiction and falls for a wealthy entrepreneur.

    Cast and Directors
  • Isla Fisher as Rebecca Bloomwood
  • Hugh Dancy as Luke Brandon
  • Krysten Ritter as Suze
  • Joan Cusack as Jane Bloomwood
  • Director: P.J. Hogan

    • Movie Review

    The most fun movie ever, I've watched it dozens of times! There was a period of time I watched it every other day! It just makes you feel so good, it's so easy to watch, the acting is great! It's like a play you just want to keep going to see. The conflicts are not super stressful so it's just such a comforting film. You can't help but love Isla Fisher she is the best.
